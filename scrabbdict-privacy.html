Privacy Policy for Scrabbdict

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Controller and contact

This privacy policy applies to the Scrabbdict mobile application ("Application") created and operated by Piotr Sochalewski. For privacy questions or requests, contact: psdevsupport@icloud.com.

Summary

The Application does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, contacts, precise location, or similar information. The Application processes limited technical and usage information for analytics and crash diagnostics.

Information processed in the Application

  • Dictionary usage data. The Application uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand basic product interaction. Custom analytics events include word check, tiles search, pattern search, selected language, and whether a checked word exists. The custom analytics events do not send the searched word, letters, or pattern text.
  • Crash and diagnostics data. The Application uses Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose crashes and improve stability. Crashlytics may process crash traces, app version, operating system version, device model, installation identifiers, timestamps, and other diagnostic information needed to understand a crash.
  • Technical analytics data. Firebase may also process technical data such as app instance identifiers, device and app information, timestamps, and event data.
  • Local app data. The Application may store app settings such as selected language on your device to provide the dictionary functionality.
  • Support communications. If you contact support by email, your email address and message are processed to respond to your request.

Information the Application does not process

The Application does not collect precise real-time location, contacts, camera or microphone recordings, advertising tracking identifiers, payment information, or account information. The Application does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Purposes and legal bases

  • Providing app functionality. Local settings are processed to provide the dictionary features you request. The legal basis is performance of the app functionality requested by you and, where applicable, legitimate interests in operating the Application.
  • Analytics and product improvement. Analytics data is processed to understand usage and improve the Application. The legal basis is legitimate interests in maintaining and improving the Application, unless consent is required by applicable law.
  • Crash diagnostics and security. Diagnostics data is processed to find and fix crashes and maintain app reliability. The legal basis is legitimate interests in keeping the Application secure and reliable.
  • Support. Support communications are processed to respond to your request. The legal basis is legitimate interests in providing support and, where applicable, taking steps requested by you.
  • Legal compliance. Data may be processed when necessary to comply with applicable legal obligations.

Third-party services

  • Google Firebase / Google Analytics for Firebase / Firebase Crashlytics. Used for analytics and crash diagnostics. More information: Firebase Privacy and Security, Firebase Crashlytics terms, and Google Analytics for Firebase terms.

Sharing and international transfers

Data is shared with the service provider listed above only as needed to provide analytics and diagnostics. Google may process data in countries other than your country of residence. Where required, transfers rely on mechanisms made available by Google, such as applicable data processing terms, standard contractual clauses, or other recognized transfer mechanisms.

Retention

Local settings remain on your device until you delete them through device controls or uninstall the Application. Support emails are retained only as long as needed to handle the request and any related follow-up. Firebase Crashlytics retains crash reports and associated identifiers for a limited period described by Firebase, currently 90 days before deletion begins. Google Analytics for Firebase data is retained according to the Firebase/Google Analytics retention settings and policies.

Your choices and rights

You can stop further local processing by uninstalling the Application. If you are in the EU/EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction with privacy rights, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, objection, or withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise rights concerning data controlled by Piotr Sochalewski, contact psdevsupport@icloud.com.

Children

The Application is not directed to children and is not used to knowingly solicit personal data from children.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data processed for the Application. No security method is perfect, but the Application limits processing to the data needed for the purposes described above.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above shows when the current version was published.
